High-Quality Custom Carpentry & Woodworking

Creekside Carpentry is a locally owned carpentry business serving Waynesville, North Carolina and the surrounding areas of Haywood and Buncombe counties. We specialize in custom carpentry, finish carpentry, built-ins, renovations, and one-of-a-kind woodworking projects designed to enhance both residential and commercial spaces.

Trusted Local Carpenter in Western North Carolina
